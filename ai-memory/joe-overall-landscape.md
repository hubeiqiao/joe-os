## English-Learning Arc

This is one of the most important threads in Joe's story.

- Joe repeatedly describes English as a long-term weakness, especially speaking.
- He uses phrases like "deaf and mute English" to describe his starting point in a small-town environment.
- He failed multiple times to improve and treated spoken English as the main obstacle to going abroad.
- IELTS 6.5 in March 2022 was good enough to unlock the Canada path, but not enough to remove the problem.
- New Zealand in 2023 gave him a confidence breakthrough after real conversations with native speakers.
- After moving to Canada, he kept using his own tools daily and became more confident, even if he did not always think the raw skill improvement was dramatic.
- A key philosophical shift came from Mark: "Language is for communication, not perfection."
- Joe Speaking exists because the problem remained real in his own life. It is not a detached startup idea.

## AI Arc

- Joe treats ChatGPT's launch on November 30, 2022 as a historic personal turning point.
- Early AI usage focused on writing, translation, learning, prompting, and practical personal workflows.
- By 2023, he was deeply engaged with GPTs, AI-assisted English learning, and reflections on how AI changes work and life.
- By 2024, he was thinking hard about AI, physical-world interfaces, user experience, and the limits of efficiency culture.
