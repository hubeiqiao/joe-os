## 📖 My Story

### 🔥 A Passion for Building

My journey began with a simple fascination: how do ideas become reality? 💡 Born in Sichuan and educated in Hangzhou, I spent eight transformative years in Beijing, where I discovered my calling at the intersection of technology and human experience. There's something magical ✨ about the moment when a concept transforms into something tangible—whether it's a smartphone interface that delights users or an AI tool that amplifies human creativity. This magic has driven every step of my career.

### 🇨🇦 The Move to Canada & New Horizons

In August 2024, I made the leap from Beijing to Ottawa—not just a geographical move, but a conscious choice to embrace uncertainty and growth 🌱. Canada represents more than a new home; it's a canvas for the next chapter of my story. This transition embodies my core belief: "Seeking a reachable future in uncertainty." Sometimes the most meaningful journeys begin when you step away from the familiar and trust that "the seemingly unrelated points in life will eventually be connected as a line sometime in the future." 🔗

### 🤖 My Philosophy: The Pathless Path

Since late 2022, I've been deeply immersed in the AI revolution—not as a passive observer, but as an active participant 🎯. I've created 21 personal GPTs, accumulating over 200,000 conversations 🗣️. But beyond the numbers, I've discovered something profound: AI doesn't replace human creativity; it amplifies it 📈. I believe we're living in an era of the "pathless path"—where traditional career boundaries are dissolving, and AI empowers us to fearlessly explore passions that transcend conventional definitions of work.

In 2025, everything sped up ⚡. AI in my hand evolved from helping me proofread, to searching and answering questions, to creating images and videos, to generating 100-page documents and polishing them into decks and websites, to enabling me to ship software for myself and others 🚀. This transformation turned me from a product person into what I call a "vibe engineer"—someone who can turn ideas into reality through conversation with AI.

The first thing I shipped was deeply personal. English had been my biggest weakness—growing up in a small town, I was essentially "deaf and mute" in English for years. After struggling through countless study sessions, I finally scored IELTS 6.5 in 2022—just enough to go abroad. In December 2023, I built an IELTS Speaking Simulator GPT and open-sourced it. It went viral on RedNote, reaching 100,000+ users 🔥. After moving to Canada in August 2024, I kept practicing daily with my own tool. Then I met Mark—a mentor who taught me real-world communication and inspired a philosophy that changed everything: "Language is for communication, not perfection."

That conviction led me to build something bigger. In 2025, I built the standalone product Joe Speaking in under 80 days with Claude Code + Opus 4.5—currently in alpha. To ship it properly, I founded Just Joe Technologies Inc.—my first company 🏢. Its tagline captures what I believe: "Building What Was Impossible Yesterday." (Read the full story →)

### 🚀 My Vision for the Future

With Just Joe Technologies as a foundation, I'm excited about what's next.

I dream of inventing "an unobtrusive pen that automatically transforms handwriting into digital objects, linking the virtual and physical worlds." ✍️ This vision captures my belief that the future lies not in choosing between digital and physical, but in creating seamless bridges between them 🌉. The key isn't to fear this change, but to embrace it as an opportunity to become more authentically human 💝.

### My Origin Story

In 2012, I went to university and studied Mechanical Design. By the end of the first semester, I realized I might not be suited for a career in the mechanical industry. Around that time, the mobile Internet was rapidly developing in China.

In April 2013, I came across the launch event of Smartisan OS by chance. I was impressed by the one-of-a-kind personality of Luo Yonghao (the founder), the user-friendly features of Smartisan OS, and the company's values. That was also the first time I learned about the "product manager" profession.

In May 2014, not willing to miss another historic moment, I travelled from Hangzhou to Beijing to watch the launch of the Smartisan T1. The T1 impressed me deeply, and I had a clear idea of my career path: go to Smartisan and make products.

Smartisan T1 - The phone that inspired my career

In July 2014, I was one of the first users to get my hands on the T1. Thanks to my PPT skills acquired during university, I created an introduction to Smartisan OS highlighting its excellent features. The CEO retweeted this long Weibo twice, with nearly 1,900 retweets and more than 4 million reads.

My Weibo post about Smartisan OS

In March 2015, while preparing for my internship, a product manager from Smartisan DM'd me on Weibo. When I arrived at the meeting, there was not only him but also the CEO — Luo Yonghao. Luo said he wanted to get to know me and asked me to create a comprehensive Smartisan OS playbook. I immediately agreed and handed over my resume, saying I wanted to come to his company as a product intern. He said, "of course, no problem."

"Turning fan energy into career reality" — This experience taught me that passion combined with action can open unexpected doors.
