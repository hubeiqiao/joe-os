## FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

### Is this free to try?

Yes. Early Access users can claim free credits to try everything - enough for a full IELTS simulation or dozens of recording sessions. Just sign in with Google to claim your credits.

### How is the IELTS/CELPIP band score calculated?

It depends on the practice mode:

**Answer details:**
- **Live Simulation:** The AI examiner evaluates your voice in real-time, following IELTS/CELPIP criteria.
- **Recording Practice:** Evaluation is based on your transcript. We provide the **transparent evaluation prompt** in settings, so you can even DIY it or see exactly how it works.

The band score is just for reference. The core focus is natural communication, not perfection.

### Can I use this on my phone?

Yes. Mobile-first design. We recommend installing it as a generic app (PWA) for the best experience:

**Mobile/PWA benefits:**
- Launch instantly from your home screen
- Works offline - practice without internet
- Full-screen immersive experience

**Install instructions:**
- iOS: Open in Safari → Tap Share → "Add to Home Screen"
- Android: Open in Chrome → Tap menu → "Install App" or "Add to Home Screen"

### I experience issues in live conversations (examiner repeats questions, examiner doesn't speak). How to fix?

The AI voice detection is sensitive. For the best experience:

**Troubleshooting tips:**
- Use a quiet place with minimal background noise
- Wear headphones; even small ambient sounds can trigger detection
- Allow microphone permissions when prompted

We're continuously improving stability. If issues persist, please report via Discord or email.

### How much does it cost?

~$1 per full IELTS Speaking simulation (12-14 minutes). Daily recording practice is even more affordable. You only pay for what you use. No subscription required.

### What's the difference between Live Simulation and Daily Recording?

**Mode comparison:**
- Live Simulation: Natural conversation with AI examiner. Track question bank topics and analyze your progress. Get immediate back and forth interaction.
- Daily Recording: Record your answers anytime, anywhere. Get feedback later. Great for practicing specific topics repeatedly or fitting practice into a busy schedule.
